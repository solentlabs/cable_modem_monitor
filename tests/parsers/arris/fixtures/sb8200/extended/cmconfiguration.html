The cable modem inside the SB8200 will automatically connect to the network. If you know the frequency and upstream channel that your cable system uses to communicate you may enter it below to help the modem find it more quickly. When the cable modem successfully connects to the network it will remember the frequency. Reboot the cable modem to initiate the new settings.
